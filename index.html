About Pocket Elixir..

WHO?

We are a group of experienced mixoligsts, who between us have over 100 years experience in making cocktails.

WHAT?

Pocket Elixir is an online library of cocktails and recipes inlcuding detailed instrcutions on how to make them.

WHEN?

During the COVID-19 pandemic, it became obvious that an online library of cocktails and recipes would be a useful tool.
