Cocktail Library

Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 50ml Bourbon
  • 10ml Sugar Syrup
  • 2 Dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Orange Twist

Method

  • Add Bourbon, bitters & sugar syrup to mixing jug with ice.
  • Stir until desired dilution is achieved.
  • Julep strain into chilled rocks glass.
  • Garnish with orange twist and serve.

Moscow Mule

Ingredients

  • 50ml Vodka
  • 20ml Lime Juice
  • Ginger Beer
  • Lime Wedge

Method

  • Fill a mule mug with ice cubes.
  • Add the vodka and lime juice.
  • Top up with ginger beer.
  • Garnish with a lime wedge and serve.

Aperol Spritz

Ingredients

  • 50ml Aperol
  • 100ml Prosecco
  • Soda Water
  • Grapefruit Wedge

Method

  • Add ice, Aperol & Prosecco to a large wine glass.
  • Top with Soda Water
  • Garnish with a Grapefruit wedge and serve.

Mojito

Ingredients

  • 50ml Rum
  • 20ml Sugar Syrup
  • 30ml Lime Juice
  • Soda Water
  • Mint Leaves & Lime Wedges

Method

  • Add all ingredients apart from soda water to a collins glass and muddle.
  • Add crushed ice and top with soda water.
  • Garnish with mint sprig and serve.

Margarita

Ingredients

  • 40ml Tequila
  • 15ml Triple Sec
  • 25ml Lime Juice
  • Lime Twist

Method

  • Add Tequila, Triple Sec & Lime Juice to shaker with ice.
  • Shake hard.
  • Fine strain into chilled rocks glass.
  • Garnish with lime twist and serve.

Espresso Martini

Ingredients

  • 20ml Vodka
  • 10ml Sugar Syrup
  • 10ml Frangelico
  • 10ml Kahlua
  • 10ml Galliano
  • 80ml Espresso coffee (cold)

Method

  • Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice and shake hard.
  • Fine strain into chilled martini glass.
  • Garnish with coffee beans and serve.

Bloody Mary
